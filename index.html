// THE ORIGIN STORY

What is Comic Con?

Born from the passion of fans worldwide, Comic Con has become the ultimate celebration of pop culture — and we're bringing that magic to Villa College.

The World's Biggest Pop-Culture Phenomenon

Comic Con (short for Comic Convention) is a multi-genre entertainment and comic book convention that celebrates everything from anime, manga, and superhero movies to esports, cosplay, and indie gaming. The original San Diego Comic-Con, founded in 1970, now attracts over 135,000 attendees annually and has spawned hundreds of conventions worldwide.

These events are where fans become heroes, where students discover passions they didn't know they had, and where the line between entertainment and education beautifully blurs.

  • ⚡ Cosplay competitions where fans become their favorite characters
  • 🎮 Esports tournaments & gaming zones with the latest titles
  • 🎨 Artist alleys, merchandise markets & exclusive collectibles
  • 🎭 Interactive panels, workshops & celebrity appearances
  • 🌍 A community of thousands united by shared passions

// THE GAME PLAN

The Campus Quest — How It Works

Every attendee becomes a hero on a quest. Register, explore 9 themed faculty stations, complete challenges, and claim your Grand Prize.

🗺️ Your Hero's Journey

Upon registration, you receive a Digital Hero Passport on our web-app. Your mission: visit all 9 Faculty Quest Stations across the campus, complete each 2-minute themed challenge, and scan the QR code to earn a digital stamp.

Complete all 9 stamps and you'll face THE FINAL BOSS — the Admissions Hub — where you verify your passport, receive personalized course counseling, and enter the Grand Prize draw!

  • 📱 Step 1: Register on the web-app and get your Digital Hero Passport
  • 🗺️ Step 2: Use the Hero Map to navigate to 9 faculty quest stations
  • 🎯 Step 3: Complete themed challenges at each station (AI hacking, mock trials, CPR drills...)
  • 📷 Step 4: Scan QR codes to earn stamps and level up your passport
  • 🏆 Step 5: All 9 stamps? Face the Final Boss at the Admissions Hub for your Grand Prize entry!

🔍 BONUS: Side Quests & Hidden Secrets

The Rogue AI Scavenger Hunt: 5 hidden golden QR codes placed in secret campus locations. Find one and earn a massive point multiplier!

Flash Polls: Hourly push notifications — "Who wins: Superman or Saitama?" Vote live and see results on the main stage!

Photo Booth Viral Loop: Share your cosplay photo tagging Villa College for a bonus stamp multiplier!
