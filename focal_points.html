The Digital Backbone — Web App Update

No more paper. We have developed a custom, mobile-first Web App to manage the entire event.

📱 The Campus Quest App

Attendees must register on the app to get their "Digital Hero Passport." This captures their Name, Email, Phone, and O/A Level status before they even arrive.

How it Works for You:

  • 1️⃣ Attendees visit your Faculty and Department Zone.
  • 2️⃣ They complete your 2-minute challenge.
  • 3️⃣ You reveal a secure QR code.
  • 4️⃣ They scan it with the app to earn a "Digital Stamp" on their passport.
The Conversion: Attendees MUST collect all stamps to enter the Grand Prize Draw at the Admissions Hub (The Final Boss). This forces 100% of motivated attendees to speak to an Admissions Counselor.
